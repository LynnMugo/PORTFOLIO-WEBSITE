About Me

I am a Certified Nutritionist and Food Science professional with a strong foundation in clinical nutrition and a passion for enhancing well-being through personalized dietary solutions. My journey has expanded into technical education and more recently into technology, where I am developing my skills in HTML and Python.

As a curious lifelong learner, I’m inspired by the intersection of science, technology, and education. Whether I'm guiding students in nutrition science or exploring how digital tools can improve access to knowledge, I remain committed to sharing ideas, building practical skills, and inspiring healthier, smarter choices in every space I serve.

I value growth, purpose-driven creativity, and making a lasting, people-centered impact on health, learning, and well-being.
